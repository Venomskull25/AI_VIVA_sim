    About VivaAI

    VivaAI is an AI-powered viva preparation tool that helps students practice oral examination questions and receive detailed, structured feedback — anytime, anywhere.

    How It Works
    1

    Enter Your Topic

    Type in any subject you want to practice — from core CS subjects like DBMS and OS to specialized domains like ML or Cybersecurity.

    2

    Get an AI-Generated Question

    The AI generates a realistic viva-style question on your topic, simulating the kind of question an examiner would ask.

    3

    Write Your Answer

    Type your answer in the text area. Try to be thorough — mention key concepts, examples, and edge cases just like in a real exam.

    4

    Receive Detailed Evaluation

    Get a score out of 10, structured feedback on what you got right, and specific improvement suggestions to build on next time.
